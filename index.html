About Us

Jonathan Valentine

    • American Boneyard

    American Boneyard is the perfect place to support Jonathan's fossil adventures. They guarantee the authenticity of the fossils they provide to you. The business sells shark teeth, mammoth bones, fossilized fish, and many more beautiful fossils. The majority of the fossils listed on the site were hand collected in the United States and meticulously documented by their team of licensed collectors and, if necessary, prepped in house.
